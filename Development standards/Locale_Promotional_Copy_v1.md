## KEY DIFFERENTIATORS (What Makes Us Different)

### 1. **Hallucination Refunds (Only Locale Does This)**

**The Problem Nobody Talks About:**
- You pay for an AI task.
- Model hallucinates (fabricates data, gets context wrong, produces garbage).
- You're out the money. The vendor blames the AI.

**The Locale Solution:**
- We detect hallucinations using health scoring.
- If detected, automatic refund (from profit reserve, not your pocket).
- If you report it, we investigate and refund.
- Transparent: you see the refund, the reason, the process.

**Copy for This:**
> "Other platforms take your money and hope for the best. Locale reimburses failures. We manage the risk so you don't have to."

### 2. **Per-User Economics (Radical Transparency)**

**The Problem:**
- Competitors hide pricing in tiers and usage bands.
- You never know what you're actually paying.
- Hidden overages, surcharges, "good-faith estimates."

**The Locale Solution:**
- Every task shows cost breakdown: model, tokens used, markup, refund reserve.
- Profit split 3-way: 1/3 reserve (your safety net), 2/3 Locale (our margin).
- No hidden costs. No surprises at billing.

**Copy for This:**
> "Pricing you can see. Economics that make sense. No surprises."

### 3. **Model Rotation & Tag-Team (User Experience Win)**

**The Problem:**
- Model gets stuck or degraded mid-task.
- Task fails. You restart from scratch. Lost context.

**The Locale Solution:**
- Health monitoring detects degradation in real-time.
- First model "taps out." Fresh model tags in (wrestling metaphor).
- Context preserved. No rerun. Zero friction.

**Copy for This:**
> "When one AI gets tired, another takes over. No interruption. No data loss. Seamless handoff."

### 4. **Verified Partners (Quality Gate)**

**The Problem:**
- Freelance platforms are full of low-quality providers.
- No real vetting. Just star ratings gamed by reviews.

**The Locale Solution:**
- Background checks (if applicable).
- Portfolio verification.
- Real client reviews (not fake ones).
- Instant removal for bad actors.

**Copy for This:**
> "Verified isn't just a badge. It means we checked. Really."

### 5. **Escrow for All Transactions**

**The Problem:**
- You pay upfront, hope the work gets done.
- Platform takes your money, passes it to freelancer.
- Dispute? Months to resolve.

**The Locale Solution:**
- Your money sits in Stripe Connect escrow.
- You confirm work is complete.
- Only then does provider get paid.

**Copy for This:**
> "Your money is safe. Released only when work is verified. That's not a promise—that's code."

## LANGUAGE & TERMINOLOGY (WHAT WE SAY VS. WHAT WE DON'T)

### ✅ Say This

- "Refund guarantee" (clear, honest)
- "Health monitoring detects degradation" (technical, accurate)
- "Model rotation / tap-out" (metaphor that makes sense)
- "Escrow-protected" (standard, trustworthy)
- "Verified partner" (implies real vetting)
- "Per-user economics" (transparent, specific)
- "Hallucination" (the real term, not "occasional inaccuracy")
- "Token usage tracking" (technical, honest)

### ❌ Don't Say This

- "AI failure" (too scary, we prevent most)
- "Artificial intelligence" (say "AI," sounds dated otherwise)
- "Machine learning model" (too academic, say "model")
- "Optimization" (overused buzzword)
- "Cutting-edge," "revolutionary," "game-changing" (hype)
- "Synergistic integration" (meaningless)
- "Best-in-class" (every platform claims this)
- "Disrupt the industry" (nobody cares)
