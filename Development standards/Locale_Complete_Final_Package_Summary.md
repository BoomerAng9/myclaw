## 💼 STORAGE DECISION: GCS + FIRESTORE HYBRID (Locked In)

### **Why This Architecture?**

**For Digital Transformation vertical:**
- **GCS** stores encrypted documents (versioning, lifecycle, audit-ready)
- **Firestore** stores metadata (searchable, indexed, audit-logged)
- **Cloud Functions** syncs automatically (OCR + extraction on upload)
- **Result:** Enterprise-grade compliance + simple developer experience + cost-effective

**Benefits:**
- ✅ GDPR/HIPAA/SOC2-ready (audit logging, encryption, access control)
- ✅ Full-text search (Firestore metadata + Algolia)
- ✅ Version tracking (GCS native + Firestore metadata)
- ✅ Secure sharing (signed URLs with expiry)
- ✅ Scalable to millions of documents
- ✅ Cost-effective (~$0.025/GB effective)

**Implementation:** 4 weeks (Week 1: foundation, Week 2: OCR, Week 3: security, Week 4: search)
