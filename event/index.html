Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Coastal Talent and Innovation Hack-A-Thon?
CTIH is a 3-day hybrid hackathon that brings together developers, designers, creators, entrepreneurs, and AI innovators. Participants collaborate in teams to build projects across four tracks: AI/ML, Web3, Social Impact Tech, and Creative Tech.
Who can participate?
Anyone with a passion for technology and innovation! Whether you're a seasoned developer, a student, a designer, or an entrepreneur — all skill levels are welcome. You can join solo and form a team on Day 1, or register with a pre-formed team.
Is the event in-person or virtual?
CTIH is a hybrid event. You can attend in person at our venue or participate fully online. Virtual participants will have access to live streams, collaboration tools, mentors, and can submit their projects remotely.
What should I bring?
Bring your laptop, charger, and creativity! We provide Wi-Fi, power strips, meals, snacks, and all the tools you need to hack. If you're attending virtually, just make sure you have a reliable internet connection and your development environment set up.
How are projects judged?
Projects are evaluated by a panel of industry judges based on innovation, technical execution, design, impact potential, and presentation quality. Each track has its own judging criteria, and special prizes are awarded for Best AI, Community Impact, and People's Choice.
